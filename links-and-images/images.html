Images

image tag takes on default dimensions of image

This page covers common image formats. However you may also be interested in links or extras

JPGs

JPGs are good for photos. However can never be transparent. Good for large color pallets.

GIFs

Good for animation. Not good for large color pallet.

PNGs

PNGs are good for icons and diagrams. Not usually for photos.

SVGs

SVGs are awesome. They scale well. Could be used in place of PNGs. Be careful if they inculde font.
